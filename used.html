• Virtual Classroom

    A student in a remote location can place him/her-self in the classroom as he/she wishes. The teacher can see the students in different places according to their location they chose to see the lecture, and with natural eye contact.

  • Video Conference

    It can replace current telecommunication means, such as telephone, 2D video teleconference, internet based 2D video teleconference. In such a use case, the participant at the other side can be seen by a large display in real size, with natural eye contact.

  • Automated Driving

    This technology can be used to enable the cars to drive without driver, when it is needed, or being remotely derived to a destination. The driver can remotely control the action of the car from a super multiview display at the office/home.

  • Tele Medicine

    Currently remote surgery is nearly possible, if the doctor is trained for using such a system, his valuable experience can save a life of a person at another place over the world.

  • Augmented Reality

    Currently automated inspection of products using image processing technology is used in industries.The same object can be seen from several sides and angles and can virtually interact with the future product and optimize the design.

  • Artificial intelligence

    Video Surveillance analysis the images, by using computer vision of algorithms, from video surveillance cameras in order to recognize humans, vehicles, objects and more.

  • Virtual Advertising

    Content provider can use this technology and edit the content of animations, movies, and TV contents with a larger degree of freedom by adding virtual advertising.

  • Virtual Stadium

    Wide motion parallax realizes deep immersive sensation to truly ultra-realistic 3D image. The audiences will feel just as if they were standing in the Olympic game stadium.

  • Party, Exhibitions

    Several contents such (Private, Cultural, Industrial contents) can be provided for commercial or private purposes, and shown for the target audience in different places
